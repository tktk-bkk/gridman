ABOUT US

GRIDMAN Inc provides business matching and consulting services to bridge companies between Africa, the Middle East countries and Japan.

Nowadays Japanese companies start seeking their business opportunities in Africa and the Middle East countries. However, many of Japanese companies don’t know the business practice in those areas and struggle with the cultural barriers. We are here for you.
We deliver a tailored service to your company.
